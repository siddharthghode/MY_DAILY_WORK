DSA Learning Flowchart — 6 months (7 phases)

Click each phase's circle to mark it done. Hover for details. This flow is tuned for placements & competitive programming.

Phase 1 — C++ Essentials & Problem Solving Basics

Easy • 3 weeks
  • Syntax, IO, control flow, functions
  • Arrays, strings, basic Math
  • Intro to problem-solving & thinking

Phase 2 — Fundamental Data Structures

Medium • 4 weeks
  • Linked lists, stacks, queues
  • Recursion & basic backtracking

Phase 3 — Trees & Graphs (BFS/DFS)

Hard • 4 weeks
  • Binary trees, BST, tree traversals
  • Graph representations, BFS, DFS

Phase 4 — Sorting, Searching & Complexity

Medium • 3 weeks
